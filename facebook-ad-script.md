# SWFT CRM - Facebook Video Ad Script & Production Guide

## Ad Overview

- **Platform:** Facebook (In-Feed Video Ad)
- **Recommended Length:** 45–60 seconds (sweet spot for Facebook engagement)
- **Aspect Ratio:** 1:1 (square) for feed, also export 9:16 for Reels/Stories
- **Target Audience:** Small business owners, service-based businesses, contractors, field service companies
- **CTA:** "Start Your Free 14-Day Trial"

---

## THE SCRIPT

> **Estimated read time: ~55 seconds at a natural, confident pace**

---

### HOOK — First 3 Seconds (CRITICAL)

**[On screen: You, looking directly at camera, confident expression]**

> "If your CRM is costing you customers, it's time to switch."

**[Visual: Bold text overlay animates in — "YOUR CRM IS COSTING YOU CUSTOMERS"]**

*Why this works: Pattern interrupt. It challenges a belief and creates immediate tension.*

---

### PROBLEM — Seconds 3–10

**[On screen: You walking or gesturing naturally, conversational tone]**

> "Most CRMs are bloated, overpriced, and built for corporations — not for businesses like yours. You're stuck paying more for tools you don't even use."

**[Visual: Quick flash of a cluttered, confusing CRM dashboard (stock image or blur) with a red "X" over it]**

---

### SOLUTION — Seconds 10–20

**[On screen: You, now slightly more energized. Hold up phone or gesture toward screen.]**

> "That's why we built SWFT — a CRM that gives you everything you actually need. On the Business plan, you get unlimited SMS messaging, unlimited AI-powered messages, and unlimited team members — no caps, no surprises."

**[Visual: Screen recording of SWFT dashboard — clean, simple, modern. Text overlay bullets appear one by one:]**
- Unlimited SMS
- Unlimited AI Messages
- Unlimited Team Members

---

### FEATURES — Seconds 20–38

**[On screen: Quick cuts between you talking and screen recordings of each feature in SWFT]**

> "You get a full CRM with customer management, quotes and invoicing, job scheduling with calendar sync, Gmail and Google Calendar integration, an AI receptionist that handles follow-ups for you, QuickBooks integration, broadcast messaging, 16-day weather forecasts for field scheduling, Google Sheets export, and dedicated support from our team."

**[Visual: As you say each feature, show a 1–2 second screen recording or screenshot of that feature in SWFT. Stack text overlays on screen:]**

1. Full CRM & Customer Management
2. Quotes & Invoicing
3. Job Scheduling + Calendar Sync
4. Gmail & Google Calendar Integration
5. AI Receptionist & Auto Follow-Ups
6. QuickBooks Integration
7. Broadcast Messaging
8. 16-Day Weather Forecasts
9. Google Sheets Export
10. Dedicated Support

*Pacing tip: This section moves fast on purpose. Rapid feature callouts create a "wow, that's a lot of value" effect. Don't pause between features — let them stack.*

---

### SOCIAL PROOF / DIFFERENTIATOR — Seconds 38–47

**[On screen: You, slower pace now, more personal]**

> "Other CRMs charge you hundreds just for SMS alone. With SWFT's Business plan at $279 a month billed annually — or try it month-to-month at $349 — you get all of this in one place. No nickel-and-diming. No add-ons."

**[Visual: Price comparison graphic — e.g., "SWFT Business: $279/mo" vs a blurred competitor stack of charges. Keep it clean, no competitor names.]**

### CTA — Seconds 47–55

**[On screen: You, direct to camera, confident close]**

> "And here's the best part — you can try SWFT completely free for 14 days. No credit card gimmicks. Just sign up, explore every feature, and see the difference yourself. Click the link below and start your free trial today."

**[Visual: Bold animated text — "14-DAY FREE TRIAL" with a button graphic pointing down. End card with SWFT logo and URL.]**

## SHOT-BY-SHOT FILMING GUIDE

| Timestamp | What to Film | Notes |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| 0–3s | You, direct to camera | Good lighting, clean background. Deliver the hook with energy. |
| 3–10s | You, conversational | Slight movement (walking, gesturing). Relatable frustration tone. |
| 10–20s | You + device/screen | Hold phone or gesture to a monitor showing SWFT. Energized. |
| 20–38s | Rapid B-roll cuts | Pre-record screen captures of each SWFT feature. You do quick voiceover narration. |
| 38–47s | You, slower/personal | Build trust. Eye contact. Speak like you're talking to a friend. |
| 47–55s | You, strong close | Confident. Clear. Point down at the end (toward the CTA link). |

## PRE-FILMING CHECKLIST

- [ ] Script printed or loaded into teleprompter app
- [ ] Screen recordings of all 10 SWFT features captured
- [ ] Clean, professional background (solid wall, office, or blurred)
- [ ] Good lighting (ring light or window light facing you)
- [ ] Phone or camera at eye level
- [ ] Audio: Use a lapel mic or AirPods — built-in phone mics pick up echo
- [ ] Wear a solid-color shirt (no busy patterns — they distract on camera)
- [ ] Film in landscape AND square framing if possible (or crop in post)

---

## POST-PRODUCTION WORKFLOW

1. **Import footage** into CapCut or Descript
2. **Cut to script** — remove pauses, "ums," and retakes
3. **Add screen recording B-roll** over feature callout sections
4. **Add text overlays** — key phrases and feature names (bold, readable)
5. **Add auto-captions** — use CapCut or Submagic for animated style
6. **Add background music** — low volume, upbeat but not distracting
7. **Export in two formats:**
   - 1:1 (1080x1080) for Facebook/Instagram Feed
   - 9:16 (1080x1920) for Reels/Stories
8. **Design thumbnail** in Canva — use a frame of you + bold text: "Your CRM Is Broken" or "Try SWFT Free for 14 Days"
9. **Upload to Facebook Ads Manager** and configure targeting per the settings above

---

## KEY REMINDERS

- **First 3 seconds decide everything.** Nail the hook. Film it 5 different ways and pick the best one.
- **Captions are mandatory.** Most people watch on mute. If they can't read it, they'll scroll past.
- **One CTA only.** Don't confuse people. The action is: start the free trial.
- **Feature stacking creates value.** The rapid-fire feature section makes viewers think "all of THAT for one price?"
- **Price anchoring works.** Mentioning that other CRMs charge more for less makes $279/mo feel like a steal.
- **The 14-day free trial removes all risk.** Say it at least twice — once in the middle and once at the close.
