Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 15, 2026

SWFT CRM ("we," "us," or "our") provides a customer relationship management platform for businesses. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Account registration data (name, email, business name)
  • Customer and job records you enter into the platform
  • Integration credentials (e.g., social media page tokens, payment processor keys)
  • Communications and messages sent through the platform

2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve the SWFT CRM platform
  • Connect your account to third-party services you authorize (Facebook, Instagram, Google, Stripe, etc.)
  • Send transactional emails and notifications
  • Respond to support requests

3. Third-Party Integrations

SWFT CRM connects to third-party platforms at your direction, including Meta (Facebook and Instagram), Google, Stripe, and others. When you connect these services, we access only the data necessary to provide the requested functionality. Each third-party platform has its own privacy policy governing their use of your data.

4. Facebook and Instagram Data

When you connect your Facebook Page or Instagram account to SWFT CRM, we may access your page messages, comments, and profile information to provide messaging and CRM features. We do not sell this data to third parties. You can disconnect your Facebook or Instagram account at any time from the SWFT Connect settings page.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using Google Firebase (Firestore). We implement industry-standard security practices including encryption in transit and at rest. We do not sell your personal information.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Cookies

SWFT CRM uses cookies and local storage to maintain your login session and preferences. We do not use tracking cookies for advertising purposes.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.
