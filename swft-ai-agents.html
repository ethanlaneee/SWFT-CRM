AI Safeguards (before sending)
Skip if the invoice is already paid
Checks the invoice record right before sending. If it's been marked paid (Stripe, Square, or manual), the reminder is skipped.
Skip if the customer promised a payment time
SWFT reads the thread. If the customer said "I'll send it Friday" or "paid via Zelle this morning," no reminder goes out.
