PRIVACY & DATA

Data Storage
Your customer data, jobs, quotes, and invoices are stored securely in Firebase Cloud Firestore with encryption at rest.
AI Processing
SWFT AI uses Anthropic's Claude to process your requests. Your data is sent to Claude only when you interact with the AI agent. Claude does not store or train on your data.
